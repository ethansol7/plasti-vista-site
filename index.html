Brand perspective

A product brand built around softness, precision, and responsible material flow.

The work is positioned as a real design ecosystem rather than a collection of isolated objects. Products, process, and local production are composed as one coherent experience.

Sol modular lighting

A modular language that feels sculptural, interior-ready, and quietly iconic.

Cylinders, translucent ribs, elbows, and luminous volumes create a family that can expand across formats without losing clarity. The result is expressive without becoming noisy.

Repeatable forms build consistency across floor, table, and sculptural typologies.

Warm diffusion and ribbed surfaces create depth instead of glare.

The collection reads as a system first, then as a set of individual pieces.

PlastiVista

A circular production ecosystem designed to feel visible, local, and beautifully legible.

Collection, preparation, extrusion, fabrication, and return are treated as one elegant environment. The infrastructure is not hidden behind the product. It becomes part of the brand's value.

Keep material closer to the communities that generate and reuse it.

Make small-scale processing feel understandable rather than industrially distant.

Return reclaimed material through products with genuine desire and presence.

Material sequence

A clear progression from discarded plastic to refined product output.

The process is shown with enough clarity to feel trustworthy and enough restraint to stay visually calm.

01

Collect

Capture discarded material early and keep value close to where it begins.

02

Reduce

Prepare the stream into smaller, controlled fragments ready for transformation.

03

Extrude

Turn reclaimed material into usable feedstock for repeatable fabrication.

04

Return

Bring the material back into daily life through objects with warmth and permanence.
